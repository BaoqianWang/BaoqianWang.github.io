Baoqian Wang (王宝乾)

I am a Ph.D. student of Electrical and Computer Engineering in the joint doctoral program between University of California San Diego and San Diego State University. My advisors are Prof. Junfei Xie and Prof. Nikolay Atanasov. My research interests include Robotics and Reinforcement Learning, Coded Distributed Computing, Unmanned Aircraft Systems, Mobile Edge Computing.

I received my M.S. degree in Computer Science from Texas A&M University Corpus Christi in 2019 and B.S degree in Prospecting Technology and Engineering from Yangtze University in 2017.
